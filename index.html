Welcome to Diesel World


Diesel Artistic Director Nicola Formichetti is coming to China to celebrate the Shanghai International Fashion Showcase with a World Wide event and he wants to collaborate with you. Diesel and Nicola are enlisting a new generation of brand ambassadors, taste makers and style influencers from China and all around Pacific areas to contribute- with strong personality and coolness- making the Diesel event and create an unforgettable fashion experience at the Diesel SIFS Grand Opening Event on 8 April in Shanghai.

Be part of the show of the world’s most iconic brands: with your help, we’re hitting “refresh” on the fashion imagery, reigniting the primary particles of Diesel’s core DNA: freedom, creation, originality, and, above all, bravery.

Go to Diesel’s stores, check your outfit and post your image on this site by using the hashtag #worldofdiesel from weibo or instagram.

Join our casting and be part of the gang!

欢迎登陆DIESEL星球


2015年4月8日，DIESEL艺术总监Nicola Formichetti将现身上海国际时装周，集结时尚先锋，玩转这一全球盛事。

为此，DIESEL和Nicola Formichetti正在中国乃至亚太地区招募新一代DIESEL的代表者、反叛的定义者与时尚的引领者，以鲜明独特的个性为上海国际时装周开幕盛典强势注入新鲜血液，创新时尚体验。

作为DIESEL时尚大典不可或缺的参与者：您将与我们共同打造全新时尚意象，点燃DIESEL内核：自由、创造、原创，以及至高无上的大胆无畏精神。
